SUMMARY OF MY FIRST DAY IN SQI

I am writing to tell you how happy I am to be part of SQI. The first day I came to SQI, I was a bit anxious of what I would face because of how people explained tech would be. I tried as much as possible to keep an open mind to embrace the journey. As a first timer, I was shy and kept my cool. I observed the environment and how things are being done in order not to flop. Some of the tutors are young so I found it hard to create a tutor-student relationship with them because I didn't know how to regard them but as time goes on, I had to put that aside and accept the fact that I'm a student. In order to learn and achieve my aim, I have to be humble and submissive. We started with HTML. The word HTML sounded familiar to me and I guessed I heard it first when I was in a computer class in school.

It's indeed a wonderful experience to be a student in SQI because of the tutor's accessibility and rapt attention to student's cooperation. They always make sure the students are carried along.
